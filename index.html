About Guru

Vid. Rithika Menon, a talented Bharatanatyam artiste and accomplished psychologist. With a strong foundation in both Art and Science, she has completed her graduation with a specialization in Performing Arts and Psychology, followed by a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology. Currently pursuing her PhD in Psychology, she brings a unique blend of artistic expression and scientific insight to her performances. Starting her Bharatanatyam journey at the tender age of 6 under the guidance of Guru Vid. Mamatha Karanth, she has achieved the esteemed Vidwat-Final certification under the guidance of Guru Vid. Hemalatha Prakash. She has graced numerous stages with her captivating performances across the Nation. Apart from Bharatanatyam, she is also a self-taught Bollywood dancer, showcasing her versatility and passion for dance.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality dance education, foster creativity, and promote cultural awareness through the art of dance.
We strive to create a supportive community that encourages students to reach their full potential and develop a lifelong passion for dance.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading dance center that celebrates the diversity of dance styles, promotes cultural exchange, and nurtures the next generation of dancers, choreographers, and dance educators. We envision a future where dance is recognized as a powerful medium for self-expression, cultural preservation, and community building.

Values

At Ritu Nritya Kendra, we value:

- Creativity and Innovation: We encourage students to explore their creativity and develop their unique style.
- Cultural Heritage: We preserve and promote the rich cultural heritage of Bharatanatyam and other traditional dance forms.
- Diversity and Inclusivity: We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and promote inclusivity in our dance community.
- Excellence and Discipline: We strive for excellence in dance education and promote discipline, hard work, and dedication.
